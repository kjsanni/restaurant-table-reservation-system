WhatsApp Booking Flow

1
Client messages salon Bot Client sends any message to your WhatsApp Business number. The bot greets and shows a menu: Book, View services, Reschedule, Pay.
2
Client selects a service Client Bot lists services with prices and durations. Client replies with a letter or number. Bot checks real-time availability.
3
Client picks a date & slot Client Bot shows open time slots for the requested date, filtered by stylist/station availability. Client selects a slot number.
4
Bot confirms booking Bot Appointment is created in the system. Bot sends confirmation with date, time, stylist, station, and total price.
5
Client pays or defers Client Bot asks if client wants to pay now (Mobile Money link) or at the salon. Payment links are sent via WhatsApp. See WhatsApp Payments.
6
Reminders sent automatically Bot 2 hours before the appointment, the bot sends a WhatsApp reminder. Client can confirm, reschedule, or cancel by replying.
