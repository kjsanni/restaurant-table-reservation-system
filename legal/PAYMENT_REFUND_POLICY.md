# Payment & Refund Policy

**Last updated:** 20 July 2026

This policy explains how payments and refunds work on the Restaurant Table Reservation System ("RTRS"), operated by **Vibespot Technologies Ltd.** (Ghana). It applies to both Customers and Tenant restaurants (Merchants) using RTRS.

## 1. Payment method

- All RTRS payments are processed in **Ghana Cedi (GHS)** through **Paystack**, our payments partner in Ghana.
- Supported channels include **Mobile Money** (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), **cards** (Visa/Mastercard), and **bank** options, depending on what the restaurant enables.
- Your financial details are entered and processed directly by Paystack under its PCI-DSS-compliant environment. RTRS and the restaurant do not store your card or Mobile Money PIN.

## 2. Who you pay

- When you pay a deposit or pre-payment for a booking, you are paying the **restaurant** (the Merchant), not Vibespot Technologies Ltd. The restaurant sets the amount, currency, and whether the payment is refundable.
- RTRS records only payment metadata (amount in GHS, status, Paystack reference) to reconcile your reservation.

## 3. Deposits & pre-payments

- A deposit may be required to secure a table, particularly for large parties, peak hours, or special events. The refundability of a deposit is shown at checkout and is determined by the restaurant's policy.
- By completing payment you confirm the amount and terms displayed before authorising the charge via Paystack.

## 4. Refunds

- **Eligibility:** Refunds are governed by the restaurant's cancellation/refund rules shown at booking. Where a booking is cancelled within the restaurant's free-cancellation window, or where the restaurant cancels, a refund may be due.
- **Method:** Approved refunds are returned to the **original payment method** via Paystack (e.g. back to your Mobile Money wallet or card). We do not issue cash or store credit unless the restaurant chooses to.
- **Timing:** Mobile Money and card refunds typically settle within **1–5 business days**, subject to Paystack and your network/bank processing times. Delays from mobile operators are outside RTRS's control.
- **No-shows:** Deposits may be forfeited under the restaurant's no-show policy. No-show records may be stored on your customer profile at that restaurant.

## 5. Failed & disputed transactions

- If a payment fails, no amount is deducted (or any temporary hold is reversed by your provider). Check your Mobile Money balance/limit or card details and retry.
- For unauthorised or disputed charges, contact the restaurant first, then **Paystack support**, and your mobile operator or bank as applicable. You may also email **legal@vibespot.tech** for assistance.
- Keep your Paystack transaction reference as proof.

## 6. Taxes & fees

- Prices shown by the restaurant are set by the restaurant and may be inclusive or exclusive of **VAT** as required by the Ghana Revenue Authority. Any RTRS platform subscription fees are billed separately in GHS and may include applicable taxes.
- Paystack may apply standard transaction fees; these are borne by the Merchant unless the restaurant passes them on as shown at checkout.

## 7. Regulatory compliance

- Payment processing complies with applicable **Bank of Ghana** directives on electronic money and payment services, anti-money-laundering (AML) obligations, and Ghana Revenue Authority tax requirements.
- RTRS maintains audit logs of payment events to support reconciliation and compliance.

## 8. Contact

- Booking/payment questions: contact the restaurant directly.
- Platform/payment assistance: **legal@vibespot.tech**
- Payments partner: **Paystack** (https://paystack.com/gh)
