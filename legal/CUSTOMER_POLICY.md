# Customer Policy

**Last updated:** 20 July 2026

This policy explains, in plain language, what you agree to when you book a table or make a payment through a restaurant that uses the Restaurant Table Reservation System ("RTRS"), operated by **Vibespot Technologies Ltd.** (Ghana).

> RTRS is the technology the restaurant uses. Your booking is with the **restaurant**, not with Vibespot Technologies Ltd. Each restaurant may have its own additional rules (cancellations, deposits, dress code) shown at booking.

## 1. Making a reservation

- You provide your name, phone number, and (optionally) email so the restaurant can confirm, remind you, and manage your booking.
- You must provide accurate contact details. Wrong details may mean the restaurant cannot reach you.
- A reservation is confirmed only when the restaurant (or the system on its behalf) marks it confirmed.

## 2. Deposits & payments

- Some restaurants require a **deposit** or **pre-payment** to secure your table, especially for large groups or peak times.
- Payments are processed in **Ghana Cedi (GHS)** via **Paystack** (Mobile Money — MTN, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money — cards, and bank channels). Your card or Mobile Money details are handled directly by Paystack; the restaurant and RTRS never see your full financial details.
- The amount charged and whether it is refundable is set by the restaurant and shown before you pay.
- A successful payment generates a reference you should keep.

## 3. Cancellations & no-shows

- If you cannot attend, cancel as early as possible through the link/message you received or by calling the restaurant.
- Restaurants may apply their **no-show policy**, which can include forfeiting a deposit. No-show history may be recorded against your customer profile at that restaurant.
- Refunds (where applicable) follow the restaurant's policy and the [Payment & Refund Policy](./PAYMENT_REFUND_POLICY.md), and are returned via the original Paystack method (e.g. back to Mobile Money).

## 4. Your data & privacy

- The restaurant is the controller of the personal data you provide for your booking. Vibespot Technologies Ltd. processes it as the platform provider under the [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) and Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).
- You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data from the restaurant, or contact **privacy@vibespot.tech** for help.
- We send service messages (confirmations, reminders, waitlist updates) by SMS/WhatsApp/email. These are transactional, not marketing.

## 5. Communications & consent

- By providing your phone number/email, you agree to receive booking-related messages. Standard network charges from your mobile operator may apply.
- Optional marketing messages are sent only with your separate consent, which you can withdraw at any time.

## 6. Liability

- RTRS is a booking technology. We are not responsible for the food, service, premises, or conduct of the restaurant, or for any loss arising from your dining experience.
- The restaurant is responsible for honouring your confirmed reservation.

## 7. Complaints

For issues with your booking or meal, contact the restaurant directly. For data-protection concerns, contact **privacy@vibespot.tech** or the **Data Protection Commission of Ghana** (https://dpc.gov.gh).

## 8. Changes

This policy may change; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.
