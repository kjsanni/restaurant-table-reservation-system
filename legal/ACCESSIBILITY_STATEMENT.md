# Accessibility Statement

**Last updated:** 20 July 2026

**Vibespot Technologies Ltd.** is committed to making the Restaurant Table Reservation System ("RTRS") accessible to the widest possible audience in Ghana, regardless of ability, device, or connection quality.

## 1. Conformance target

We aim to meet the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA** for the RTRS web application. The frontend is built with Vue 3, PrimeVue, and Vuestic UI, and applies a centralised, high-contrast brand design system (WCAG-compliant colour contrast, readable typography, and consistent focus states).

## 2. Measures we take

- Semantic HTML and ARIA labels on interactive components and navigation.
- Keyboard-operable navigation, including a collapsible sidebar with visible focus indicators.
- Sufficient colour contrast and a unified design-token system for text and surface colours.
- Accessible form inputs with associated labels and error messages.
- Responsive layout usable on mobile, tablet, and desktop — including lower-bandwidth mobile networks common in Ghana.

## 3. Known limitations

Some legacy third-party components and dynamically generated content may not yet fully meet Level AA in every state. We are actively working to remediate these.

## 4. Feedback & contact

If you encounter an accessibility barrier or need information in an alternative format, contact us:

**Vibespot Technologies Ltd.** (Ghana)
**accessibility@vibespot.tech**

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days and to propose a solution where applicable.

## 5. Enforcement procedure

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the National Council on Persons with Disability (Ghana) or the relevant equality/accessibility authority in your jurisdiction.
