# Tenant (Merchant) Policy

**Last updated:** 20 July 2026

This policy applies to restaurants and other businesses ("you", "Merchant", "Tenant") that use the Restaurant Table Reservation System ("RTRS"), operated by **Vibespot Technologies Ltd.** (Ghana), to manage reservations, customers, staff, and payments. It is read together with the [Terms of Service](./TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md) and the [Data Processing Agreement](./DATA_PROCESSING_AGREEMENT.md).

## 1. Your role as a data controller

When you collect and process personal data of your guests and staff through RTRS, **you are an independent data controller** under Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843). You are responsible for:
- Having a lawful basis to collect each guest's name, phone, email, and reservation history.
- Giving your guests a privacy notice (you may reference our [Customer Policy](./CUSTOMER_POLICY.md) and [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md)).
- Honouring data-subject rights (access, correction, deletion) for your own guest data.
- Setting appropriate data-retention periods in your RTRS settings.

Vibespot Technologies Ltd. acts as your processor for that data and will assist you on request.

## 2. Account setup & users

- You must provide accurate business details, including your registered business name and, where relevant, your Ghana Revenue Authority (GRA) VAT/registration details for invoicing.
- You are responsible for the roles, permissions, and staff accounts you create. Onboard only authorised staff and remove access promptly when staff leave.
- You are liable for all activity under your Tenant account.

## 3. Taking payments (Ghana)

- RTRS supports payments in **Ghana Cedi (GHS)** through **Paystack** (Mobile Money, cards, banks). You must connect your own Paystack account/keys in Settings and ensure your payout bank account is a valid Ghanaian account.
- You are responsible for the prices, deposits, taxes (including VAT where applicable), and refund rules you configure. RTRS only records metadata; settlement and payouts are handled by Paystack to your account.
- You must comply with **Bank of Ghana** directives on electronic money, payment services, and anti-money-laundering, and with Ghana Revenue Authority tax obligations.
- See the [Payment & Refund Policy](./PAYMENT_REFUND_POLICY.md) for how refunds and disputes are handled.

## 4. Customer communications

- You may send booking confirmations, reminders, and waitlist messages through RTRS. These are transactional.
- Marketing messages to your customers require their consent; you must honour opt-outs.
- You are responsible for the content of messages you send and for complying with Ghana's electronic communications rules (e.g. the Electronic Communications Act, 2008 / Act 775 and the Data Protection Act, 2012).

## 5. Fees & subscription

- Plan fees are billed in GHS via Paystack on a recurring basis. Taxes (e.g. VAT) may be added as required by law.
- Failure to pay may result in suspension after any grace period. You remain responsible for guest data and must export it before closure if needed.

## 6. Acceptable use

You agree not to use RTRS for unlawful activity, to process data without a lawful basis, to spam customers, or to circumvent tenant isolation. We may suspend accounts that violate Ghanaian law or these terms.

## 7. Support & contact

For onboarding and operational support, use the in-app help or contact **legal@vibespot.tech** for contractual matters.

## 8. Changes

We may update this policy; continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
