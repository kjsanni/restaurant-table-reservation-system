WhatsApp Payment Flow

1
Bot offers payment options Bot After booking (or on demand), the bot asks: Pay now via Mobile Money, Card, or pay at the salon?
2
Client picks MoMo provider Client MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, or 9PSB — all supported under Central Bank of Nigeria directives.
3
Bot sends Paystack payment card Paystack A rich payment card appears in WhatsApp showing amount, service, and a Pay button. Powered by Paystack (NGN).
4
Client taps & approves Client Client taps the button, Paystack opens a MoMo prompt on their phone, client enters PIN to approve the charge.
5
Payment confirmed via webhook Bot Paystack webhook confirms payment. Bot sends a WhatsApp receipt with transaction ID. Appointment status updates to "Paid" in the dashboard.
6
Deposit & partial payments Bot Salons can require a deposit (e.g. NGN 30 to confirm color appointments). Bot collects the deposit first, balance paid at salon or via second link after service.
